How I got into Touhou

Back in 2011 to 2012 I became obsessed with Synthesia videos. Eventually I came across the Death Waltz video (which is actually a black MIDI of Last Brutal Sister Flandre S.) and the Necrofantasia MIDI. I thought they sounded cool but didn't really care about where did they come from.
Over time, in 2013, I learned that Death Waltz was actually a remix of a song called "U.N. Owen was Her?" from Touhou 6. I also saw black MIDIs of Night of Nights and Bad Apple, both also from the same series. I learned that Touhou was actually game series by ZUN, so I got EoSD, downloaded a spanish patch and tried it out.
Now you have me here obsessing over fictional women lol.

By the way I only found out several years later that Last Brutal Sister Flandre S. was also based on NO REMORSE from Ketsui and Hibachi from Daioujou.
